Stainless Steel, Metal, Wood Work and Fabrications in Sri Lanka

We undertake all types of stainless steel, wood, and metal fabrications and customized items. We also modify and repair any type of machinery. Service provided islandwide across Sri Lanka.

Stainless Steel Fabrication in Sri Lanka

Customized kitchen equipment, stainless steel doors, hotel laundry equipment, handrails, counters, shelves, sinks, trolleys, and durable stainless steel solutions made to your exact measurements and finish requirements.

Wood Working

Custom display tables, display racks, customized chairs, wood lamps, cupboards, wall panels, countertops, partitions, and premium wood finishes designed for homes, showrooms, offices, and commercial spaces.

Metal Working

Metal fences, gates, moulds, brackets, frames, grills, supports, structural fabrication, and custom metal work for residential, commercial, and industrial projects with strong welding and clean finishing.

Machinery Modifications

Imported a machine but it’s not suitable for your workflow? We can modify, upgrade, rebuild, and fine-tune it to fit your needs, improve safety, increase efficiency, and ensure smooth operation with reliable spare solutions.

Conveyor Systems

Conveyor system design and fabrication for factories and warehouses, including belt conveyors, roller conveyors, custom frames, supports, guards, and layouts to match your production line, space, and load requirements.

Repairs & Maintenance

We undertake repairs and services for most types of equipment, machinery, fence doors and many more. Contact us with your requirement; we will evaluate and quote.
